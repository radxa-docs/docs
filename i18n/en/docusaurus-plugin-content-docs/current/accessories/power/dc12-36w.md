# Radxa DC12 36W Power Adapter

The Radxa DC12 36W power adapter is specifically designed for Radxa SBC products. It provides up to 3A of current at 12V to meet the power requirements of Radxa SBCs with multiple connected peripherals. It is compatible with Radxa ROCK 5T and other Radxa SBCs, as well as Radxa CM3/3S/3I, CM4, and CM5 baseboards.

![Power DC12 36W](/img/accessories/power/power-dc12-36w.webp)

## Product Specifications

- Connector Specification: 5525, with an outer diameter of 5.5mm and an inner diameter of 2.5mm
- Power Input: 100-240V ~ 50/60Hz 0.8A MAX
- Power Output: 12V / 3A 36W
- Certifications: UL / CE / FCC / CCC
- Polarity: Inner positive, outer negative
