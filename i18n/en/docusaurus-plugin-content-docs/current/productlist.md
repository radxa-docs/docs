# Radxa Products

Welcome to the Radxa Product Center! Below are our main product families to help you choose the right product for your needs.

## How to Choose the Right Product?

- **Entry-level AI & General Computing**: Consider the ROCK 3 or ROCK 4 Series
- **High-performance AI & Multimedia**: Check the ROCK 5 Series
- **Industrial & Embedded Applications**: Browse the Computer on Module Family
- **Network & Edge Computing**: Refer to the Network Computer Family
- **Specific Application Scenarios**: Explore specialized products like X Series, NIO Series, etc.

> 💡 **Tip**: Each product page includes detailed specifications, quick start guides, and compatible accessories information.
